ScanHawk Enterprise: Effortless Absence Tracking

ScanHawk Enterprise

New

Welcome to ScanHawk Enterprise, a revolutionary system designed to simplify the process of logging attendance through an intuitive NFC-card system. Say goodbye to manual registers and hello to streamlined, real-time tracking!

Key Features

  • Effortless Attendance Logging: Simply scan your NFC card to log in or out.
  • Real-Time Data: Instantly register and view attendance data, anytime, anywhere.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed to be intuitive and hassle-free for all users.
  • Secure: Only authorized NFC cards can interact with the system, ensuring safety and reliability. Password integrated and local database encryption, with secure access for admin users.
  • Coming: Future integration with TeamPortal for seamless integration with the ScanHawk system.

Why Choose ScanHawk?

ScanHawk isn't just about logging attendance – it's about enhancing your workflow and giving you a tool that actually works. With seamless integration, easy setup, and reliable performance, it’s the future of absence tracking. Period.

With ScanHawk Desktop you also get a PHP document to easily share the data on your local network -- And it's read-only! So there's no need to worry about anybody tampering with your data.
